This Data sample comprises 105 records (Table 1). The price ranged from approximately $160,000 to 920,000 and the average selling price is close to $360,000.

About 65% of the houses come with a pool (n = 67), and about 75% come with a garage (n = 78).

2. Correlated Variables Highlights
